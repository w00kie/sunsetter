How Sunsetter works

The instant the Sun meets the horizon

We show the dates when that lower edge just touches the horizon along your chosen sightline. In the calculation, the Sun’s center is 0.25° above the geometric horizon.

This is an astronomical alignment. Local terrain, buildings, weather, and atmospheric effects can change what you actually see.
